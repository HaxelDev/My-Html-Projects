Blastoise

Type: Water

Attacks:

  • Hydro Pump
  • Water Pulse
  • Ice Beam

Description: Blastoise is the final evolved form of Wartortle. It is a Water-type Pokemon known for its immense water cannon on its back and powerful water-based attacks.

Additional Information about Blastoise

Blastoise is a formidable Water-type Pokemon. Here are some additional information about this Pokemon:

  • Height: 1.6 meters (5 feet 3 inches)
  • Weight: 85.5 kilograms (188.5 pounds)
  • Ability: Torrent
  • Habitat: Lakes and coastal waters
  • Blastoise's water cannons can shoot water with great force, allowing it to launch powerful attacks.
