Venusaur

Type: Grass / Poison

Attacks:

  • Vine Whip
  • Razor Leaf
  • Poison Powder
  • Solar Beam

Description: Venusaur is the final evolved form of Bulbasaur. It is a dual-type Pokemon, Grass and Poison, known for its large flower on its back and powerful grass-based attacks.

Additional Information about Venusaur

Venusaur is a majestic Grass-type Pokemon. Here are some additional information about this Pokemon:

  • Height: 2.0 meters (6 feet 7 inches)
  • Weight: 100 kilograms (220.5 pounds)
  • Ability: Overgrow
  • Habitat: Forests and grasslands
  • Venusaur's flower on its back releases a pleasant aroma when it blooms.
