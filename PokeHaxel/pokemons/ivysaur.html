Ivysaur

Type: Grass / Poison

Attacks:

  • Vine Whip
  • Razor Leaf
  • Poison Powder

Description: Ivysaur is the evolved form of Bulbasaur. It is a dual-type Pokemon, Grass and Poison, known for the large plant bulb on its back.

Evolves into: Venusaur

Additional Information about Ivysaur

Ivysaur is a strong Grass-type Pokemon. Here are some additional information about this Pokemon:

  • Height: 1.0 meters (3 feet 3 inches)
  • Weight: 13 kilograms (28.7 pounds)
  • Ability: Overgrow
  • Habitat: Forests and grasslands
  • Ivysaur's plant bulb on its back grows larger as it evolves and gains more power.
