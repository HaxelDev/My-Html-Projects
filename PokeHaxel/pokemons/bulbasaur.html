Bulbasaur

Type: Grass / Poison

Attacks:

  • Vine Whip
  • Razor Leaf
  • Poison Powder

Description: Bulbasaur is a dual-type Pokemon, Grass and Poison. It has a plant bulb on its back that grows as it evolves.

Evolves into: Ivysaur

Additional Information about Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a unique dual-type Pokemon. Here are some additional information about this Pokemon:

  • Height: 0.7 meters (2 feet 4 inches)
  • Weight: 6.9 kilograms (15.2 pounds)
  • Ability: Overgrow
  • Habitat: Forests and grasslands
  • Bulbasaur is one of the starter Pokemon in the Kanto region.

Evolution:

Evolves from Bulbasaur at level 16.

Evolves from Ivysaur at level 32.

This is the final evolved form of Bulbasaur.
